Momo,
that’s my pet name but for me, she isn’t just a regular pet. She’s my best friend, my enemy, and my everything. In December 2020, in Muak Lek, Thailand, there was an event market. It's kinda traditional event , which the local people call a cowboy market. While walking around, looking for something good stuff, my boyfriend and I were caught checking on a pet vendor. To be honest, Momo was not the pet that I wanted to buy. There’s another rabbit that looked more stunning to me. Of course, the price was even more expensive than other rabbits. So, I asked my boyfriend, I call him “Oppa” (Em, I have been addicted to Korean drama Korea but unfortunately my boyfriend is from Cambodia. He is a good guy. I should feel honored to have him but, hmm that will be another story). So, I asked him to choose which one he felt was a good one to buy. He kept pointing at Momo. I still remembered Momo was the only rabbit that we carried among other rabbits. I asked my boyfriend, “Why her?” And he told me, “I don’t know, I just feel that she is special”. At first, I tried to confront him to buy another rabbit. Unfortunately, in the end, I agreed to adopt Momo. I still remembered how happy I was, especially since that was my first pet and first rabbit. I have never seen any rabbits before (back when I was in Malaysia).

Why is Momo special? Well, you can say she is just a normal rabbit. But for me, she’s my strength, power, and she is like my own daughter. I named her Momo because, at that time, I was nuts over Chinese drama. And the heroine's name was Sutomo, but called by nickname Momo. Therefore, I decided to name her after the heroine in that drama.

Currently, I am a university student staying in a dormitory. I believe most dormitories have their own rules. For me, we are not allowed to have pets. Yea, I know you can say I disobeyed the rules. Well, I’m staying alone and don’t really like to socialize with almost anybody. With Momo coming into my life, I felt like everything was amazing. My dark life seems to be bright because of her.

Momo and I always have ups and downs. I really love her. How silly am I, bringing her everywhere. I still remembered bringing her, traveling around. I usually put her in a bag which was a little difficult for her to breathe. The trip took 2 hours from my campus to the place that we wanted to go. Just halfway, I can see that Momo was so weak and couldn’t breathe. So I tried to hug her and amazingly she calmed down and went inside my jacket. At first, I was scared if she might fall down because we were using a bike, not a car. Unexpectedly, she prefers to stay inside my jacket instead of the bag. You must wonder why a rabbit can fit into my jacket. Well, Momo back then was just 2 or 3 months old so she’s super small. After we arrived at the destination, we put her on the grass. I can see she was so excited but what made my heart melt was even though she was so excited, she always looked behind and made sure I was still there. What a wonderful memory I had with her.

During my semester class session from January until May 2021. Wow, that was super stressful to me. Having an online class, I think anyone can be related to how stressful it was. So lucky I was, Momo always stayed under my feet. Sometimes she bit me as she wanted to say “Mommy, go rest, give me some love and attention!”. Around March I guess, the Covid-19 in Thailand was getting better; so we were allowed to have face-to-face class again. At first, I was so worried about Momo. Luckily not every class that I have to meet in class. So I still have more time with Momo. Whenever class ended, I always rushed to see Momo. Whenever I went out of my room, she always stayed in front of the door waiting for me to come back. There’s a lot of time I caught her waiting for me in front of the door. When she saw me, she would run away under the bed and only show her head with the look on her face. It seems like she wanted to tell me, “Ahh you’re back, where have you been, “ (hahaha). Every time I went inside my room and wanted to hug her, she always bit me, sat so far away, and just looked at me as if she hated me so much. But I just take it as that is how she showed her love to me. I always counted until 2 minutes and she would come to me by herself and kiss me.

Talk about the bed. In the daylight, Momo always took over my bed. For your information, I gave freedom to Momo to do whatever she wanted to do. I mean I didn’t put her in the cage because I didn’t want her to stress. People say rabbits can have a long life if they feel love and happiness. Our afternoon sleep time was so memorable to me. We love to listen to Chopin until we fall asleep. At night, Momo always stayed under my feet. The most amazing feeling I ever felt was when I spent my time on my phone either for drama or games. I loved the feeling when she walked on my blanket and bit me as if she was telling me to stop playing games. Then she put her head on my shoulder. I understood what she wanted; so I did as she wanted, I stopped the things that I was doing then I hugged her and gave her a massage. Wow, I missed the moment. As she realized I stopped playing with my phone, she would go back to under my feet and sleep there. When I went to the toilet, she would look at me as if she was asking “Where do you think you are going” and I always replied, “No, I just go to the toilet”. Momo sometimes waited for me either in front of the toilet door or just sitting on the bed where my pillow was. She was so loyal to me, she would sit and wait for me until I came back. Then she went back to sleep at her spot. Our morning routine, to be honest, Momo was kinda annoying. Around 5 am every day, she always woke me up either by eating my hair or climbing the table and threw everything until I woke up. My biggest regret, sometimes when I felt I wanted to continue sleeping and felt annoyed with her, I would switch off the aircon and put her on the balcony until I fully woke up. So she had to stay on the balcony alone until 10 am or 11 am.

Well, at first Momo had a bad habit where she liked to pee and poop in front of the door. I can say that I don’t like messy rooms since I am too lazy to always clean it up. Because Momo came into my life, I changed my routine. Every time I woke up, I needed to clean the floor so when others came they would not know about Momo. In my first 5 months with Momo, I was kinda struggling to put her on the balcony every 10 pm because every night the RA would check the room. We always had an argument. Whenever I don’t play with her when it’s time to play, she would start biting me. The funny part, I would bite her ear back.

In July 2021, I realized something happened to her. She didn’t want me to carry her and she would come climbing to be hugged and kiss me. I also realized she would go to the balcony every 9:40 or 9:50 pm by herself without me having to force her. She started to pee and poop on the toilet. At first, I was amazed and worried but also thanked her for doing that; so it became easy for me to clean the room. Our sleep routine was still the same and I really appreciate our time together. On July 16, 2021; I was sick because I just got my first dose of vaccination Covid-19. She behaved so well that day. She slept beside me and kissed me. I remembered that time telling her “Momo if mommy dies please be a good girl”. I can see her eyes were crying and started hugging her and kissing me more. After that day, our day became so harmonious. We played around, she liked jumping around and running around.

On July 27, 2021, I woke in that morning, around 5:53 am. We spent our time together. I was taking a short video with her. I didn’t know why but I kept saying “Momo, mommy loves you and mommy is going to miss you”. Every time I leave the room I will always say three times, “ Momo, mommy loves you, I love you”.

0n August 6, 2021; Friday morning, I woke around 11:00 am. I realized Momo was biting a pillow’s cotton as well as her own fur. I was worried at first but when I saw her playing around made me think, “Momo, you’re so funny”. I tried to take that thing from her but she didn’t allow it. Around 1:00 pm, I realized Momo didn’t eat or drink anything. She pulled her fur out and kept it inside the pillow. I thought that was kinda cute then I played around with her for a while. I saw her fur on the bed, I tried to throw it away but she took it from my hand and put it back inside the pillow.

A few hours later, I realized her stomach had become small. Well, she’s kinda fat because she ate every moment. I tried to pull her hair, but instead of biting me, she was showing signs of being in pain. I told my boyfriend about it and he told me that I should not be worried because it happens 2 times a year. It’s already 5:00 pm and Momo still has not eaten or drunk anything. She just stayed on the balcony. I tried to carry her to go inside. I was really worried about her because normally she would bite me if I tried to carry her but this time she just showed signs of being in pain. Around 8:00 pm and 10:00 PM, I was busy with the zoom meeting with my church and family members from Sabah, Malaysia. Momo suddenly came to me that night. She came from the balcony and ran to me and wanted me to hug her. But I rejected her because I was in a zoom meeting. She then went back to the balcony. I was so worried about her but I needed to close the door because it was almost time for RA to do a room check. After the room, the check was done, and I carried Momo to go inside the room. She was so weak at that time. She just ran and sat on her toilet. I tried to carry her to sleep together but she didn’t want to. At that time, I had a feeling that might be the last time I would see her.

The next morning, I realized Momo had been sitting at the balcony door since night. The moment I opened the door, she just laid down like bread and one foot came out. She was so weak. I called my boyfriend. He told me that I was overreacting and gave Momo to him. I was so worried and asked him to send her to the vet. Well, we are in quarantine; so we are not allowed to go outside as we want. When I tried to put Momo in a pet bag, Momo hugged me with a sign of pain. I can understand she said, “don’t please”. After passing Momo to him, I kept calling him asking how Momo. I even cursed him such as “let’s break up, you don’t even care about her, give her back to me, if Momo dies I will hate you forever”. My boyfriend felt annoying and closed the call. I kept calling him and he didn’t answer my call. About 4:00 pm, my boyfriend sent me the message “love, you can hate me forever.” I didn’t reply to his message but instead, I called him immediately. I was asking him what happened and he told me, “Momo already passed away”. It turned out that he brought Momo to the vet without telling me. He told me Momo screamed and died at the vet.

I didn’t blame him, if only I had forced him to send Momo on that Friday, maybe she would still be alive. I did think if only he told me when he went to the vet. Momo might me survive or not, at least I want Momo to pass away under my hugs. To be honest, I felt like I wanted to die with her. I always thought I would die first before her. She was just 8 months old, and she has gone so fast. Today, August 9, 2021; life was super empty without her. Every time I stay in my room; I can hear her running around. I feel like I want to end my life. For other people, they might think I am overreacting, but for people like me, who do not socialize with other people, do not have friends, Momo is everything to me.
